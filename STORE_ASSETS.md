# 🚀 HunterOS - Store Launch Strategy

This document outlines the metadata, compliance rules, and visual strategy for deploying **HunterOS** to the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

## 🏷️ Brand Identity
- **Product Name**: HunterOS
- **Official Tagline**: "AI-Assisted Career Execution."
- **Keywords**: AI Job Search, Career Optimization, Resume Tailoring, Application Tracker, Professional Growth.

---

## 📸 Screenshot Strategy
*Goal: Showcase Value + Trust.*

1. **The Hero**: Dashboard overview with "Mission Control" heading. (Indigo glow).
2. **AI Tailoring**: A split view showing a generic resume becoming a "Job-Specific PDF."
3. **Smart Analytics**: Charts showing "Interview Rate" and "Platform Success."
4. **Agent Feed**: Live logs showing the system identifying and scoring jobs.
5. **Universal Sync**: A montage of the Phone + Desktop working together.

---

## ✍️ Listing Copy

### 📌 Title (30 chars)
HunterOS: AI Career Assistant

### 📝 Subtitle (80 chars)
Optimize your job search. Tailor resumes. Track success in real-time.

### 📄 Short Description (80 chars)
HunterOS is your personal AI career assistant. Manage your job search like a pro.

### 📄 Long Description
Take the stress out of the job hunt. HunterOS uses advanced AI to help you find, tailor, and track your applications from one unified command center.

**Core Features:**
- **AI Resume Tailoring**: Automatically optimize your profile for every specific role.
- **Smart Analytics**: Understand your conversion rates and identify what works.
- **Mission Control**: Monitor your job search activity in real-time.
- **Cross-Platform Sync**: Manage your career from your phone or desktop.

## 🔐 Compliance & Safety (IMPORTANT)
To avoid rejection from Apple/Google, follow these positioning rules:

| ❌ AVOID (Red Flags) | ✅ USE (Safe) |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Auto-apply bot" | "AI-assisted application management" |
| "LinkedIn Scraper" | "Professional network data analysis" |
| "Automates your account" | "Simplifies application tracking" |
| "Bot that applies for you" | "Intelligent career search assistant" |
